Polkadot development is on track to deliver the most robust platform
for security, scalability and innovation.

True interoperability

Polkadot enables cross-blockchain transfers of any type of data or asset, not just tokens. Connecting to Polkadot gives you the ability to interoperate withwide variaty of blockchains in the Polkadot network.

Economic & transactional scalability

Polkadot provides unprecedented economic scalability by enabling a common set of validators to secure multiple blockchains. Polkadot provides transactional scalability by spreading transactions across multiple parallel blockchains.

Easy blockchain innovation

Create a custom blockchain in minutes using the Substrate framework. Connect your chain to Polkadot and get interoperability and security from day one. This ease of development helps Polkadot'snetwork grow.

Forkless and future-proof

Polkadot can upgrade without hard forks to integrate new features or fix bugs. This capability enables Polkadot to easily adapt to changes and upgarde itself better technologies become available

Security for everyone

Polkadot's novel data availability and validity scheme allows chaine to interact with each other in a meaningful way. Chains remain independent in their governance, but united in their security.

User-driven network governance

Polkadot has a sophisticated governance system where all stakeholder have a voice. Upgrades to the network are coordinated on-chain and enacted autonomously, ensuring that Polkadot's development reflects the values of the community and avoids stagnation.

Powering the polkadot network

The Dot token serves thre distinct purposes:
governance over the network, stalking and bonding.

Governance

Polkadot token holders have complete control over the protocol. All privileges, which on other plateforms are exclusive to miners, will be given to the Relay Chain participant (DOT holders), including managing exceptional events such as protocoal upgrades and fixes.

Stalking

Game theory incentives oken holders to behave in honest ways. Good actors are rewarded by this mechanism whilst bad actors will lose their stake in the network. This ensures the network stays secure.

Bonding

New parachains are added by bonding tokens. Outdated ornon-useful parachain are removed by removing bonded tokens. This is a form of proof of stake.
